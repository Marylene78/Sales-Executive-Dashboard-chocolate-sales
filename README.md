# Sales-Executive-Dashboard-chocolate-sales


## Project Summary

This project involves the creation of a Power BI dashboard to evaluate global sales performance for a chocolate manufacturer. The analysis processes transactional data to identify key revenue drivers, regional market trends, and sales representative effectiveness. The final output is an interactive single-page report that translates raw data into strategic business intelligence.

 Key Insights

* **Top Performers:** Individual sales analysis identified **Ches Bonnell** as the lead contributor by revenue.
* **Product Demand:** **Smooth Silky Salty** is the primary revenue generator, while **50% Dark Bites** leads in total shipment volume, indicating high market penetration.
* **Geographic Trends:** Regional preferences vary significantly, with **Eclairs** performing best in India and **50% Dark Bites** leading in the Australian market.
* **Sales Seasonality:** Historical data shows peak sales activity occurred in **January 2022**, providing a benchmark for seasonal forecasting.

 How to Use the Dashboard

1. **Global KPIs:** Observe the top Header Cards for an immediate view of Total Revenue.
2. **Filter by Dimension:** Use the **Country** and **Product** slicers on the left panel to filter all visuals for specific market segments.
3. **Temporal Analysis:** Utilize the **Date Slider** to narrow the analysis to specific months or quarters.
4. **Performance Deep-Dive:** Interact with the **Top Sales Person** bar chart to see the specific product mix and regional distribution of any individual representative.
5. **Comparative Analysis:** Use the **Product by Country** chart to compare regional performance across different product lines simultaneously.
